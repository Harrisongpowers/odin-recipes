  • 1 1/2lb baby yellow potatoes (It muy impotante there are the same amount of potatoes as that big hunka meat)
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil dizzled accross that skillet
  • 3 Tbps butter... Divided
  • 5 Garlic cloves, mince those bad boys down

Stepperoons

  1. To prepare the garlic butter steak recipe with potatoes wedges: In a large bowl, combine the steak strips with soy sauce, olive oil, pepper, and hot sauce. Set aside and allow steak strips to marinate while you cook potatoes.
  2. Oh yeah its getting real. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, mix 1 tablespoon olive oil and 1 tablespoon butter. When butter is melted, add potatoes wedges. Cook for about 4 minutes, stir and cook an additional 4-5 minutes until potatoes wedges are golden and fork-tender. Transfer sauteed potatoes to a plate and set aside.
